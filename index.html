Introduction

I am PhD student in Astrophysics at the University of Oxford and avid Trekkie. While joining the crew of the Enterprise would be my dream for the moment I work on 'ground-based Star Trek', that is studying exoplanets from Earth using telescopes.

Research

  • The Reflected Light of Exoplanetst

    We can learn a lot about a planet from the light it reflects from the properties of clouds to the presence of rainbows.

  • Observations

    I observe close in giant planets to study their reflection at high spectral resolution using a technique called High Resolution Cross-Correlation Spectroscopy.

  • Towards Earth-like Planets

    I simulate observations of next generation telescopes to see what we might learn about potentially Earth-like in the coming decades.
