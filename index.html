Introduction

I am PhD student in Astrophysics at the University of Oxford and avid Trekkie. While joining the crew of the Enterprise would be my dream for the moment I work on 'ground-based Star Trek', that is studying exoplanets from Earth using telescopes.

Research

  • The Reflected Light of Exoplanets

    We can learn a lot about a planet from the light it reflects from the properties of its clouds to the presence of rainbows.

  • Observations

    With ground-based observatories we can study the reflection of close-in giant planets at high spectral resolution using a technique called High Resolution Cross-Correlation Spectroscopy.

  • Towards Earth-like Planets

    With detailed simulations, we can discover what we might learn about potentially Earth-like in the coming decades with next generation telescopes.
